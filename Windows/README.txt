##################################
#####                        #####
#####  Jumping With Friends  #####
#####                        #####
##################################

Jumping With Friends is a unique two player head-to-head competitve soccer game. Unlike normal soccer, players perform flips through the air and diving headers while completely limited to two controls: TILTING and JUMPING. Gone are the days where soccer involved running, slide tackling, and large fields! Test your skill against friends and strangers in this chaotically ridiculous game.

Game Controls:

Player 1
-- W = Jump
-- A = Tilt Left
-- D = Tilt Right

Player 2
-- Up Arrow = Jump
-- Left Arrow = Tilt Left
-- Right Arrow = Tilt Right
